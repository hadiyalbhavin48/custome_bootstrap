Long-Term Freelance Coaching

Looking to start or improve your freelance career?

I'm Elisabeth Mayr, a freelance UX/UI web and mobile designer. Let's work together

Freelance coaching can be as flexible as you need it. Depending on your availability and your goals, you can start with weekly, bi-weekly or monthly sessions. If you’re unsure, you can also [book a chemistry call] to see if it’s something you can commit to regularly. Whatever you decide, the overall objective is the same: to help you achieve your goals.

As your coach, I will help you to help yourself in becoming a successful freelancer. Together, we’ll define your goals and create a bespoke roadmap with actionable steps while setting realistic time frames.

From there, I’ll guide you along the way with practical advice and lessons to help you in achieving these goals successfully.

So, if you find yourself struggling to make a start or don’t know which way to turn in your freelance career, coaching can have incredible benefits.

Long-Term Freelance Coaching

Understanding your freelance business

As an experienced freelancer, I know that getting caught up in too much client work will make it easy to lose sight of the overall goal of the business.

A freelance coach will get a birds-eye view of your business and find areas to improve and automate, so you can concentrate on what you do best: Deliver great work and help your clients achieve their goals. It’s about stepping back before stepping up. As your business coach, I will help you evaluate what works and what doesn’t. From there, you’ll see what actions to take to make your goals attainable.

Doing this allows you to see all the moving parts of your business. You’ll learn how to take control of your freelance career, enabling you to make any changes along the way to grow your understanding of how everything works - from the ground up.

Generating a plan

Once you understand where you want to be, it’s time to start creating a plan. Coaching calls will provide simple but effective ways to make a business plan that will help achieve your goals in a realistic timeframe.

Improving confidence

Established freelancers will know that finding your confidence when things aren’t going as well as you’d hoped can be challenging.

Regular coaching will help you maintain and manage your motivation and inner strength through any situation, the good and the bad.

Marketing yourself

As a freelancer, you are the be-all and end-all of the business. You need to know how to market yourself and your work in a way that makes clients want to work with you.

You can learn how to market yourself effectively to reach your main audience and attract your dream clients. Learn what works, what doesn’t and how you can implement marketing strategies to get your business thriving.

Effective communication

All freelancers need to know how to communicate with clients to understand their needs effectively. You also need to know how to negotiate timeframes and costs.

In the coaching sessions I will help you understand these lines of communication and how to navigate them better as a freelancer.

Overcoming procrastination

Every freelancer, no matter where they are in their career, has struggled with procrastination. The reason is often caused by the uncomfort that comes with a task and the underlying fear of not being good enough. Add a sprinkle of imposter syndrome and you have a cherry on top.

Coaching can help you get into the right mindset to stop procrastinating and start getting things done. You’ll learn to take on bite-sized chunks that are attainable within the timeframe.

Setting boundaries

Many freelancers put in a lot more hours than the average 9-5. But you don’t have to. If you manage your time well and set boundaries, you’ll be able to create a work-life balance that works for you. It’s common in self-employment to work longer hours to achieve success but it's all down to how you manage your business and your freelance career. There will be phases where you might end up working more, but you don’t have to make this your default state.

However, it becomes an issue when the lines between personal and professional time blur. Coaching sessions can show you how to establish boundaries and stick to them—giving you the time you need for yourself while also reaching your professional goals.

FAQs

Freelance coaching is for people who are starting their freelance career as well as any freelancer who wants to take their business to the next level. Whether you’re trying to improve your workflows or want to grow in areas you’ve already achieved, there is always something to learn.
