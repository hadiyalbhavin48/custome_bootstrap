Here s how we ll work together

As a freelance UX designer, I can work closely with my clients throughout the entire process, ensuring the final product exceeds your needs and expectations.

Discussion

Let's chat! I'll grasp the essence of your project and learn about your goals, so I can consult for the ideal approach, whether you're starting from scratch or refining existing concepts.

Research & Analysis

Where possible, I analyse your current products data to see where the user experience can be refined. I ll have a closer look at competitors and review the areas of improvement.

Concept Development

Once we get an overview of where the real problem lies, I ll work on user flows based on your businesss goals and requirements while focusing on the user's needs. After the basic flows are confirmed, I'll design wireframes to ensure we re on the same page.

Designing & Prototyping

See your product come to life. Using Figma, I will create pixel perfect prototypes to bring your products vision into reality

Testing

Before handing things over to developers, I conduct qualitative user research, ensuring the designs are ready for the real world. Here I delve into user insights and refine interactions where needed.

Hand-off documentation

If developers weren t already involved from the start to ensure the designs created can actually be implemented, I ll create a cleaned up version of the final designs, filled with annotations to hand over to your developers.
