About The FC

The Fashion Conscious - short 'The FC' - is an online marketplace for sustainable fashion. Different to other marketplaces, the FC isn't about generating sales but aims to help the shopper make sustainable choices. The website is currently under development.

I was the sole UX Designer on this project and collaborated with a Visual Designer who ensured that the website was representing the FC brand properly.
