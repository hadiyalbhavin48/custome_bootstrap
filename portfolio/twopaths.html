Twopaths

Branding for a Freelance Consulting Team

Role

Graphic Designer

Duration

3 weeks

Tools

Photoshop, Power Point, Illustrator, InDesign

Summary

Branding for a Consultancy Team

Client

Two Paths

The Task

Karen and Steve are freelance consultants who offer their services individually but also work as a team. They were looking for a branding that works well for their purposes when they promote themselves in a group or individually.

Research

To find out what direction Karen and Steve wanted to go with their branding I created some moodboards with example designs I found online. Based on the designs they liked and further questions I asked, it became clear that they were looking for a modern look which wasn't too trendy and something that shows their personality.

The Idea

The client has made it quite easy for me by choosing a company name that was simple to play with: Twopaths. Suggesting several ideas, the one they liked the most was to have their handwriting used for the logo.

Execution

One part of the logo was drawn by Steve, the other by Karen. I digitalised them and created some combinations and had them choose their favourite. I then added colour, blue was suitable because it represents seriousity, trust and integrity - all attributes Karen and Steve wanted to represent.

The only thing left was combining their new logo with a typeface that doesn't interfere with the handwriting. I placed a few suitable options next to the logo and narrowed it down for Karen and Steve to choose what they felt represented them best.
